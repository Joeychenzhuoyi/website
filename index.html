A journey to Korea!

South Korea, in East Asia on the southern Korean Peninsula, has a prime geographical location bordered by North Korea and surrounded by seas. It boasts amazing tourist spots like Hallasan Mountain for nature lovers, Gyeongbokgung Palace for history seekers, and Everland for family fun. When it comes to food, the national dish kimchi, sizzling bulgogi, and flavorful bibimbap offer a delicious taste of Korean culture, making it a must-visit destination.
